Data on the go

Take your ERP with you. EasyButton is designed for use on desktop and mobile. Looking up information from your phone or tablet is a breeze, whether or not your desk is close by.

This is not an ERP

EasyButton is built on Epicor*. It does not replace Epicor*.

Data is pulled from ERP databases (such as the Part master record and the Supplier Price Lists **). EasyButton's web panel will reflect live changes made in Epicor*, and can even help clean up or solve data discrepancies as they appear.

Create useful reports

Easily search and filter data, then export it with a single click to CSV or Excel. This is an incredibly useful tool for analyzing statistics, sharing data with third-party companies and contractors, and generating reports on product lines and business activities.

  • Export a vendor's product line and send it to them for more information
  • Analyze the total worth and distribution of your stock
  • Create a workbook of an entire product line and find data errors
  • Download, modify, then re-upload data via DMT*
